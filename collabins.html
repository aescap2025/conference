DTU
Delhi Technological University (DTU) stands as a premier institution with a legacy of over 80 years in engineering, technology, and interdisciplinary education. Originally established in 1941 as Delhi Polytechnic, later known as Delhi College of Engineering, DTU has evolved into a hub of cutting-edge research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.

Ranked 6th by India Today, 35th in engineering by NIRF, and within 601-800 globally by Times Higher Education, DTU’s research excellence is reflected in its impressive Scopus h-index of 97. Its Innovation and Incubation Foundation (IIF) fuels over 50 startups annually. Spanning 164 acres, the Rohini campus is home to 14,000 students, including a diverse international community from 50+ countries.

With stellar placements, a global alumni network, and a commitment to inclusivity, DTU seamlessly blends its rich heritage with modern innovation, reinforcing its status as a world-class technological powerhouse.

DDUC
Deen Dayal Upadhyaya College (DDUC), a distinguished constituent college of the University of Delhi since 1990, epitomizes excellence in inclusive education and holistic development. Named after the revered philosopher-politician Deen Dayal Upadhyaya, the college moved to its state-of-the-art, eco-friendly Dwarka campus in 2020, integrating green initiatives like rainwater harvesting and solar power.

Its 7.7-acre campus boasts cutting-edge laboratories, a 42,000-volume Wi-Fi-enabled library, a 300-seat auditorium, and sports facilities including squash courts and an archery range. Committed to inclusivity, DDUC offers scholarships, anti-ragging initiatives, and outreach programs such as NSS campaigns for underprivileged communities.

By blending academic rigor with cultural enrichment, DDUC fosters an environment where students thrive in a transparent, merit-based ecosystem. With an award-winning faculty fostering an interactive learning experience and strong support for research, innovation, and community outreach, DDUC remains a beacon of holistic education and future leadership.
