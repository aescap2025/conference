Welcome to AESCAP 2025

The Academic Exchange and Student Conference on Advancements in Physics (AESCAP) is an annual conference for physics students, organized by Local Committee Delhi, TC India of the International Association of Physics Students (IAPS).

The inaugural edition of AESCAP is set to take place from 9 to 12 April 2025 at our collaborating institutions: Delhi Technological University (DTU) and Deen Dayal Upadhyaya College (DDUC).



Hurry up, as registrations close on 31 March 2025 at 11:59 PM IST.
