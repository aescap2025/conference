Welcome to AESCAP 2025

        

    The Academic Exchange and Student Conference on Advancements in Physics (AESCAP) 
    is an annual conference for physics students, organized by Local Committee Delhi, TC India of the 
    International Association of Physics Students (IAPS).
    


    The inaugural edition of AESCAP is set to take place from 9 to 12 April 2025 
    at our collaborating institutions: Delhi Technological University (DTU) and 
    Deen Dayal Upadhyaya College (DDUC).
    


    Registrations are open for students currently pursuing a Bachelor’s or Master’s 
    degree in Physics or a related discipline. The conference will 
    feature an exciting lineup of activities, including:
    
    
        
  • Student Talks & Poster Presentations
    • 
        
  • Guest Lectures & Panel Discussions
    • 
        
  • Hands-on Workshops & Networking Sessions
    • 
        
  • Social Events & Interactive Activities
